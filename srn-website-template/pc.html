About PC/Desktop

Desktops are made of many different components that all have they're own purpose.

This is a general purpose computer that is made up of separate things that are needed. A PC is less expensive than a laptop but is not portable as they are made of separate components meaning you will need to copy files if you want to move it elsewhere if necessary.. All the components are connected by wires which can clutter the desk space.
